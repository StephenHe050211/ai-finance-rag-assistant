An AI assistant used in finance should separate retrieval, reasoning, and final answer generation. It should not invent numbers when the source document does not contain the answer. It should show which source snippets were used.

For private companies, a local or offline model can reduce data leakage risk. However, the system still needs access control, logging, validation, and escalation rules. High-risk outputs such as settlement amounts, regulatory filings, or client-facing messages should require human approval.
